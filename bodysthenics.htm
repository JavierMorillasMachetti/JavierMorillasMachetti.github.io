What is it Bodysthenics?

Bodysthenics is a Streetworkout/Calisthenics academy which strives to create a new concept of fitness whereby every client shall grow into a stronger version of themselves through the understanding of their own body strength (sthenos). This bodyweight training movement will create the ideal path for any individual (from all fitness backgrounds and all levels of ability) to build the ultimate understanding of their own physical and mental strengths and abilities through calisthenics. All fitness experts at Bodysthenics are committed to helping every client achieve their own fitness goals and tailor all programs strictly around the individuals ability and chosen paths. WE COMMIT to creating a friendship and everlasting bond with every client and make a solid effort in understanding every individuals needs and shape their training around their chosen pathway using bodyweight elements and movements. Everyone here at Bodysthenics is considered our streetworkout family and we offer to be more than just the average fitness instructor but more so a mentor and the ultimate motivation to strive for greatness, by using the laws of inertia and defying the laws of gravity !
